2. **Phase 2: Animation Preparation**  This is a critical distinction in our methodology. Many developers would embed the video directly, resulting in a slow, frustrating user experience. Our image sequence technique, which converts the video into a series of lightweight images, guarantees a  **"super smooth scroll-based animation on the web"**  that loads instantly, reflecting the premium quality of your product and respecting your customer's time.  
3. **Phase 3: AI-Agentic Development**  The term "AI agent" is key: unlike a simple code generator, Anti-Gravity plans, executes, and problem-solves. If the scroll animation feels "too fast" or "jumpy," we describe the issue in plain English, and the AI agent implements the fix in minutes, not days.
